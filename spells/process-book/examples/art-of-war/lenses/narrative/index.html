      Front matter

      The Thirteen Chapters

      About this lens. The text is Lionel Giles' 1910 scholarly translation of Sun Tzŭ on the Art of War, sourced from Project Gutenberg. Sun Tzŭ's verses appear in the primary serif track; Giles' framing notes appear in the secondary track, and when you toggle "Close reading" any bracketed commentator quotations he gathered from Ts'ao Kung, Tu Mu, Chang Yu, Mei Yao-ch'en and others surface as a separate stack. Cross-references inside notes (for example XI.30 linking to VI.21) are clickable and jump straight into that verse.

      Chapter numbers use Giles' Roman numerals. Verse numbers follow Sun Hsing-yen's paragraph divisions, which Giles preserved. Depth levels 1 → 4 correspond to book map → chapter → verse → commentator stack.
