The Art of War

Sun Tzŭ · Translated by Lionel Giles, 1910

The Art of War is a 6th-century BCE Chinese military treatise. Across thirteen aphoristic chapters, Sun Tzŭ lays out a complete philosophy of conflict — planning, terrain, morale, deception, and espionage. Lionel Giles' 1910 translation weaves in commentary from eleven Chinese scholars spanning two thousand years, making the book a living conversation between generals and philosophers.
